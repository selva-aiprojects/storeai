---
description: how to manage home delivery customers (Home Delivery Workflow)
---

This workflow manages the lifecycle of customers who require products delivered to their home address.

### 1. Register a Delivery Node (Customer)
- Navigate to **"Delivery Nodes"** in the sidebar.
- Click **"New Customer"**.
- Enter the name, phone, and **Primary Home Address** (including City and Zip).
- Once committed, this customer is available for all future sales.

### 2. Processing a Home Delivery Sale
- Navigate to the **"Sales Terminal"**.
- Click **"New Entry"**.
- Select the **Customer Identity** from the dropdown. This will automatically pull their saved address.
- Check the **"Home Delivery Protocol"** box.
- Verify the delivery address or modify it if they want delivery to a different location.
- Commit the sale.

### 3. Fulfillment & Bill Delivery
- Go to the **"Sales Terminal"** table.
- Identify the sale (Marked as "HOME DELIVERY").
- Click **"Log Shipment"** once the package is ready.
- Attach the **Tracking Number** and **Carrier**.
- Generating the Invoice PDF will now include the **Home Delivery Protocol** and destination address on the bill.
