description: how to manage employee presence and performance (Attendance & Performance Workflow)
---

This workflow tracks the operational health of your workforce and their performance ratings.

### 1. Daily Attendance Protocol
- Navigate to the **"Workforce Portal"** in the sidebar.
- Locate the target employee in the roster.
- Click the **"Presence"** button to log their attendance for the current timestamp.
- *Intelligence Sync*: Attendance logs help HR determine payroll eligibility and reliability metrics.

### 2. Performance Evaluation
- Hover over the **Performance** column in the workforce table.
- Use the **Rating Protocol** to assign a valuation from 1 to 5 based on their operational output:
    - **5 Stars**: Exceptional throughput, zero errors.
    - **3 Stars**: Standard operational output.
    - **1 Star**: Critical performance gap.
- Performance ratings are visible to Managers and Admins to aid in promotion and bonus decisions.

### 3. Integrated Payroll Resolve
- High attendance and high performance metrics are automatically used to validate the **Salary Disbursement (Payroll)** process.
- To disburse a salary, click the **Hand/Coins 💰 icon** on the employee row.
- This creates a **Ledger Debit** and pays the employee for the selected month.
