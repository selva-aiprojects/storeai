---
description: how to add an employee to the system (Staffing Workflow)
---

To register a new member of your workforce, follow this procedure:

1. **Establish User Connection (Optional)**:
    - If the employee needs to log into the system, first create a **User Identity** (see System Access workflow).
    - If they are a non-system employee, skip to Step 2.

2. **Register Personnel**:
    - Navigate to the **"Workforce Portal"** in the sidebar.
    - Click **"New Entry"** in the top header.

3. **Input Artifacts**:
    - **System User Link**: Select the email of the user identity you created in Step 1 (or "None").
    - **Operational ID**: Enter a unique employee ID (e.g., EMP-105).
    - **Designation & Department**: Assign their title and workspace node.
    - **Salary**: Enter their annual valuation.

4. **Finalize**:
    - Click **"Commit Protocol"**. The employee will now appear in your payroll and attendance logs.
