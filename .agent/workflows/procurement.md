---
description: how to order products from a vendor (Procurement Workflow)
---

Follow these steps to replenish your inventory from strategic partners:

1. **Verify Stock Health**:
    - Navigate to the **"Catalog Matrix"** in the sidebar.
    - Identify items with the **"REORDER"** status (Stock ≤ Reorder Point).

2. **Initiate Procurement**:
    - Go to **"Supply Chain"** in the sidebar.
    - Click the **"New Entry"** button in the top header.
    - Select your **Target Supply Partner**.

3. **Build the Manifest**:
    - Select the artifacts (products) you wish to order.
    - Specify the quantity. The system will automatically pull the unit cost from your catalog.
    - Click **"Commit Protocol"** to issue the Purchase Order (Status: PENDING).

4. **Inward Logistics (Warehouse Arrival)**:
    - Once the physical stock arrives at your warehouse, go back to the **"Supply Chain"** table.
    - Locate your P.O. and click the **"INWARD"** button.
    - The system will instantly increment your inventory counts and mark the order as **"RECEIVED"**.
